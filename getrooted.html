Are you ready?

1. Sign up Online

If you haven’t done so already, please sign up by using the form online.  We will send out monthly reminders to keep you on tract to finish the Challenge.

SIGN UP NOW

2. Join a Group

Joining a group will help you succeed in this challenge.   We encourage you to form groups of about 4 – 6 people. 

3. Find a partner

Within your group pair yourself with someone else, and make each other accountable. This will help you stay on track, encouraging one another to keep going!

4. Read Daily

Get your Bible ready! Complete your Bible reading according to the day’s reading schedule.

5. Check in Weekly

We encourage you to get together with your group once a week in order to check on each other, pray and encourage one another.

6. Turn in Monthly

We will provide you with a printed out monthly reading schedule, so you can easily keep track of your reading.
