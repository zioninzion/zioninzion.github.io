About

I Am a man made in three parts—spirit and soul and body—Gen. 2:7; 1 Thes. 5:23!

I Believe that* (and am convinced that it is demonstratively true that):

  • God is real (Acts 14:17; Rom. 1:19-20) and Triune—Gen. 48:15-16; Isa. 6:8; Matt. 28:19; 2 Cor. 13:14.

  • God-breathed words were compiled to be the Bible—2 Tim. 3:16-17.

  • God has an economy—1 Tim. 1:4; Eph. 1:5, 9-10; 3:9-11.

  • God became a man—Jesus Christ—John 1:1, 14, 17.

  • Jesus Christ died for my sins and rose on the third day—1 Cor. 15:3-4.

  • Anyone who believes into the person of Christ becomes

    • justified before God—Gal. 2:16.

    • born of God with His divine, eternal life—John 1:12-13; 3:16.

    • a member of the Body of Christ—the (universal) church—Rom. 12:5; Eph. 1:22-23.

  • All the believers in Christ in a city constitute a local church—Acts 8:1; 13:1; Rev. 1:11.

  • All the elect of God will be the New Jerusalem for eternity—Eph. 1:4; 5:27; Rev. 21:2.

I Call upon Jesus as the Lord to enjoy Him—Rom. 10:12; Gen. 4:26!

I Discovered the meaning of my human life—Gen. 1:26; Rom. 8:29!

I Exercise my spirit to live Christ and build up the church—1 Tim. 4:7; Gal. 6:18; Phil. 1:21; 1 Cor. 14:4, 32!
