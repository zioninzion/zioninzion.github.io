About

Based on the self-authenticating nature of the Bible, I hold it to be the God-breathed word, necessary and sufficient to make one fully equipped for every good work—2 Tim. 3:16-17.

I am a man made in three parts—spirit and soul and body—Gen. 2:7; 1 Thes. 5:23.

I cannot deny that God is real—Acts 14:17; Rom. 1:19-20.

I am certain that this God Triune—Gen. 48:15-16; Isa. 6:8; Matt. 28:19; 2 Cor. 13:14.

I am persuaded that this Triune God has an economy—1 Tim. 1:4; Eph. 1:5, 9-10; 3:9-11.

I affirm that in accordance to His economy He became a man—Jesus Christ—John 1:1, 14, 17.

I believe that this One died for my sins and rose on the third day—1 Cor. 15:3-4.

I have found the meaning of my human life—Gen. 1:26!
