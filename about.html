About

I am a man made in three parts—spirit and soul and body—1 Thes. 5:23.

I love Jesus Christ the Triune God-man (the Father—Isa. 9:6; the Son—Luke 1:32; the Spirit—1 Cor. 15:45; man—1 Tim. 2:5).

I aspire to live according to the economy of God—1 Tim. 1:4.
